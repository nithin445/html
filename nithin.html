Prehistoric Creatures

Explore the fascinating creatures that roamed the Earth millions of years ago.

Tyrannosaurus Rex

The T. Rex was one of the most fearsome predators of its time, with powerful jaws and a massive body.

Triceratops

Triceratops was a large herbivorous dinosaur known for its three distinct facial horns and large frill.

Stegosaurus

Stegosaurus had distinctive back plates and a spiked tail. It was a herbivore that lived in the late Jurassic period.

Woolly Mammoth

The Woolly Mammoth was a large elephant-like animal that roamed the Earth during the Pleistocene Epoch.
